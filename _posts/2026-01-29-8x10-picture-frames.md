## Key Features to Consider:

- **Glass vs Plexiglass**: Real glass offers clarity and scratch resistance, acrylic plexiglass lighter and shatter-resistant for kids' rooms
- **Mat Board Options**: White mats provide 5x7 opening in 8x10 frame creating border, matless uses full 8x10 for larger photos
- **Frame Material**: Solid wood premium and durable, composite wood budget-friendly, metal modern aesthetic with thin profile
- **Display Versatility**: Easel backs enable tabletop display, sawtooth hangers for walls, some frames include both mounting options
- **Multi-Pack Economics**: Individual frames cost $12-25 each, sets of 3-8 reduce per-frame cost to $5-10 for gallery walls
- **Profile Depth**: Standard 0.75-1" profiles suit photos, deep 1.5-2" profiles accommodate canvas or thick matted artwork
- **Color Coordination**: Black frames universal and modern, white brightens walls, wood tones add warmth, metallics create elegance

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
