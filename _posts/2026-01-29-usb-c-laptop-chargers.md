## Key Features to Consider:

- **Wattage Requirements**: 30-45W for Chromebooks and tablets, 65W for MacBook Air/13" laptops, 100W+ for MacBook Pro 16" and gaming laptops
- **GaN Technology**: Gallium Nitride chargers run 50% smaller and cooler than traditional silicon, offering compact high-power charging in portable sizes
- **Port Configuration**: Single-port delivers full wattage, multi-port splits power intelligently, desktop stations suit permanent desk setups with multiple devices
- **PD Protocol Support**: USB Power Delivery 3.0/3.1 ensures compatibility and optimal charging speeds, look for PD certification for guaranteed performance
- **Foldable Plugs**: Compact chargers with folding prongs prevent bag damage during travel, fixed plugs offer more stable connection for desktop use
- **Cable Inclusion**: Some include USB-C cables (verify length and wattage rating), others require separate cable purchase for specific devices
- **Safety Features**: Quality chargers include over-current, over-voltage, over-temperature, and short-circuit protection preventing device damage
