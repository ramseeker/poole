## Key Features to Consider:

- **Processor Performance**: Apple M4/M5 chips lead efficiency, Intel Core Ultra and AMD Ryzen 7000+ offer Windows power, while Celeron/Pentium suit basic tasks
- **RAM Requirements**: 8GB minimum for multitasking, 16GB recommended for productivity, 32GB+ for content creation, gaming, or professional applications
- **Storage Capacity**: 256GB SSD acceptable for cloud users, 512GB-1TB ideal for local files, while eMMC storage in budget models offers limited space
- **Display Quality**: FHD (1920x1080) standard for clarity, 2K/4K for professionals, OLED for vibrant colors, with 120Hz refresh for smooth scrolling
- **Battery Life**: Chromebooks offer 8-12 hours, Windows laptops 6-10 hours, while MacBooks lead with 15-24 hours depending on usage and model
- **Operating System**: macOS for Apple ecosystem, Windows 11 for versatility and gaming, Chrome OS for simplicity and cloud-based work
- **Portability vs Performance**: 13-14" models balance mobility and power, 15-17" provide larger screens and better cooling for demanding tasks

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
