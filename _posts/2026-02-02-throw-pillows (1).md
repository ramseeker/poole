## Key Features to Consider:

- **Size Standards**: 18x18 inches suits most sofas and beds, 20x20 offers larger impact, 12x20 lumbar pillows provide back support.
- **Fill Type**: Down alternative offers plush softness without allergens, polyester fiberfill provides budget-friendly comfort, memory foam gives supportive firmness.
- **Cover Material**: Cotton breathes well and washes easily, velvet adds luxury texture, linen provides casual elegance, faux fur creates cozy ambiance.
- **Closure Style**: Hidden zippers create seamless appearance, envelope closures allow easy insert removal, button closures add decorative detail.
- **Color Coordination**: Neutral colors (beige, gray, white) coordinate universally, accent colors (navy, rust, sage) add personality and depth.
- **Pattern Mixing**: Combine solid colors with patterns, vary scale of patterns, maintain consistent color palette for cohesive design.
- **Seasonal Rotation**: Light colors and breathable fabrics for spring/summer, rich jewel tones and plush textures for fall/winter months.

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
