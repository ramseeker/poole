## Key Features to Consider:

- **Blade Material**: Stainless steel standard for durability, titanium-bonded stays sharp 3x longer, carbon steel offers professional sharpness
- **Handle Design**: Straight handles suit most tasks, bent handles keep fabric flat for sewing, ergonomic grips reduce hand fatigue
- **Size Selection**: 5" for detail work and kids, 8" standard office and craft size, 8.5-9" for fabric cutting and heavy-duty tasks
- **Blade Sharpness**: Micro-tip for precision cutting of intricate patterns, standard tips for general use, blunt tips safety for children
- **Specialized Features**: Serrated edges grip slippery materials, smooth blades create clean cuts, decorative edges for crafting
- **Ambidextrous Use**: True ambidextrous scissors work equally for left and right hands, many "universal" scissors favor right-handed users
- **Maintenance**: Quality scissors stay sharp for years with occasional professional sharpening, dishwasher-safe kitchen shears convenient

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
