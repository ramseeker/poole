## Key Features to Consider:

- **Burn Time**: Ranges from 40-150+ hours depending on size
- **Wax Type**: Soy wax is cleaner burning; paraffin is more traditional
- **Scent Throw**: Premium candles have stronger fragrance that fills rooms
- **Jar vs. Pillar**: Jar candles are safer and easier; pillar candles are decorative
- **Wick Type**: Wood wicks crackle; cotton wicks burn quietly

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
