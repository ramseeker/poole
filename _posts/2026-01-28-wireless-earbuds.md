## Key Features to Consider:

- **Noise Cancellation Technology**: Active ANC blocks external sounds for immersive listening, while transparency modes let you hear surroundings when needed for safety and awareness
- **Battery Life Expectations**: Entry models offer 20-30 hours total, mid-range 40-60 hours, premium 70+ hours including case charging for extended use between charges
- **Sound Quality Profiles**: Look for aptX, LDAC, or spatial audio support for high-fidelity listening, with EQ customization options to match your music preferences
- **Fit and Comfort Design**: Choose between traditional in-ear, earhook sports designs, or open-ear bone conduction based on activity level and comfort preferences
- **Water Resistance Ratings**: IPX4 handles sweat and light rain, IPX5-7 suitable for workouts, IPX8 allows submersion for swimming and extreme conditions
- **Connectivity and Compatibility**: Bluetooth 5.3+ ensures stable connections, while ecosystem integration (Apple H2, Samsung Galaxy) provides seamless pairing and features
- **Microphone and Call Quality**: Multiple mics with AI noise reduction ensure clear calls, essential for work-from-home and business communication needs

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
