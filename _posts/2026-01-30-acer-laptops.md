- **Product Line Strategy**: Swift ultraportable premium, Aspire budget mainstream, Predator high-performance gaming, ConceptD professional creators

- **Display Technology**: Swift Edge pioneered 16" OLED ultrabook category, Predator high-refresh gaming panels, ConceptD color-accurate Pantone validated
- **Build Quality**: Swift aluminum chassis, Aspire plastic construction, Predator gaming-grade cooling, ConceptD professional white design
- **Cooling Systems**: Vortex Flow fans on Predator, TwinAir on Swift models, standard cooling on Aspire, ConceptD precision thermal
- **Eco Innovation**: Aspire Vero uses 30% post-consumer recycled plastic, Earthion recycling programs, sustainable packaging materials
- **Warranty**: 1-year International Travelers Warranty standard, extended warranties available through retailers and Acer
