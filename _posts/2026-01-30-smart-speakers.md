## Key Features to Consider:

- **Voice Assistant Choice**: Alexa supports 100,000+ skills and wide device compatibility, Google Assistant excels at search and Android integration, Siri optimal for Apple ecosystem
- **Sound Quality Tiers**: Budget speakers adequate for voice commands, mid-range offers good music playback, premium delivers hi-fi audio with spatial/Atmos support
- **Smart Home Integration**: Alexa/Google compatible with most brands, HomeKit requires Apple-certified devices, Zigbee/Matter hubs enable direct device control
- **Display Options**: Smart displays add video calling and visual information, touchscreens enable easier control, audio-only speakers more affordable and focused
- **Multi-Room Audio**: Sonos industry-leading for whole-home sync, Alexa/Google groups work across brands, Apple AirPlay 2 for iOS ecosystem
- **Portability**: Battery-powered speakers enable outdoor use, water-resistant ratings (IPX4-IPX7) for poolside, most smart speakers require constant power
- **Privacy Features**: Mute buttons disable microphones physically, camera shutters on displays, indicators show when listening/processing commands

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
