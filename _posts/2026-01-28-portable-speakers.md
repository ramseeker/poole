## Key Features to Consider:

- **Sound Quality vs Portability**: Larger speakers (JBL Charge, Bose SoundLink Max) deliver fuller sound, while compact models (JBL Go, Clip) prioritize convenience
- **Battery Life Requirements**: Budget speakers offer 10-15 hours, mid-range 20-24 hours, while premium models can exceed 30 hours on single charge
- **Water Resistance Ratings**: IPX4 handles splashes, IPX7 allows submersion for 30 minutes, with floatable designs ideal for pool and beach use
- **Connectivity Features**: Bluetooth 5.0+ ensures stable 60-100ft range, TWS stereo pairing creates surround sound, PartyBoost connects multiple speakers
- **Power and Volume**: Watts indicate loudness (5W for personal, 20-30W for rooms, 50-100W for parties), with distortion-free max volume varying by design
- **Additional Features**: Built-in powerbanks charge devices, LED lights enhance parties, microphone inputs enable karaoke, EQ customization via apps
- **Durability Considerations**: Rugged designs with shock-resistant casings, dustproof ratings (IP6X), and durable materials ensure longevity for outdoor adventures

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
