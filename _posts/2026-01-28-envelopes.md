## Key Features to Consider:

- **Business Envelopes**: Security tinting provides privacy; peel & seal is more convenient than gummed
- **Invitation Envelopes**: Match envelope size to card size (A7 for 5x7, A6 for 4x6)
- **Chinese New Year**: 2026 is Year of the Horse; traditional red color symbolizes good luck
- **Bulk vs. Small Packs**: Bulk (500+) offers better value; smaller packs (50-100) for occasional use
- **Poly Mailers**: Waterproof and tear-resistant; perfect for e-commerce shipping
- **Budget Envelopes**: Cash envelope systems help with financial planning and saving challenges
