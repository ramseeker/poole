- **Product Line Hierarchy**: Zenbook premium thin-and-light, VivoBook mainstream value, ROG gaming performance, ProArt professional creators
- **OLED Display Advantage**: ASUS leads with OLED across price points offering 100% DCI-P3 color, infinite contrast, Pantone validation on ProArt
- **Cooling Systems**: ROG Intelligent Cooling with liquid metal, standard models adequate fans, ProArt precision thermal for sustained workloads

- **Battery Life**: Zenbook 12-18 hours productivity, VivoBook 8-10 hours, ROG 4-6 hours gaming, larger batteries in 15-16" models

- **Warranty**: 1-year standard international, AccidentalDamage Protection available, ROG Premium Care for gaming laptops
