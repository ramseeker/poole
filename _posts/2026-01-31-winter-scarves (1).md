## Key Features to Consider:

- **Material Warmth**: Cashmere warmest and softest but delicate, wool durable natural insulation, acrylic affordable easy-care alternative, fleece athletic performance
- **Size & Length**: 60-70 inches standard wrap-around length, 80+ inches for multiple loops, infinity scarves eliminate ends, oversized 50x50+ doubles as shawl
- **Weight Options**: Lightweight scarves layer under coats, medium-weight versatile year-round, heavyweight chunky knits maximum warmth
- **Care Requirements**: Cashmere dry clean or hand wash delicate, wool hand wash cold, acrylic machine washable, fleece easy machine care
- **Style Versatility**: Solid colors match any outfit, patterns add visual interest, neutral tones (grey/black/camel) most practical
- **Functional Features**: Hidden pockets secure valuables, convertible styles (scarf to infinity), neck gaiters pull over face, fringe adds decorative element
- **Budget Considerations**: Acrylic $10-25 great value, wool blend $30-60 mid-range, cashmere $50-200+ luxury investment, cashmere-feel alternatives $15-25

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
