## Key Features to Consider:

- **Profile Height**: Low-profile caps offer modern casual styling, while high-profile structured caps maintain traditional baseball aesthetics.
- **Bill Shape**: Curved bills provide classic look and sun protection, flat bills offer contemporary streetwear styling popular with younger demographics.
- **Closure Type**: Snapback adjustable fits most sizes easily, fitted caps require exact sizing, and elastic/velcro closures offer convenience.
- **Material**: 100% cotton breathes best in warm weather, polyester blends dry quickly for athletic use, mesh backs provide maximum ventilation.
- **Construction**: Structured caps maintain shape through wear, unstructured designs offer relaxed comfort and pack flat for travel.
- **Crown Depth**: Deep crowns accommodate more hair or larger heads, shallow crowns provide sleeker profile and fit.
- **Washing**: Hand washing preserves shape and color best, though many cotton caps withstand gentle machine cycles.

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
