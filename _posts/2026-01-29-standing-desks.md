## Key Features to Consider:

- **Height Range**: Ensure desk adjusts low enough (24-28") for sitting and high enough (48-52") for standing based on your height
- **Weight Capacity**: Budget desks support 110-176 lbs, mid-range 200-250 lbs, commercial-grade 300-355 lbs for dual monitors and equipment
- **Motor Quality**: Single motors adequate for basic use, dual motors provide faster adjustment and higher capacity, triple motors for L-shaped desks
- **Desktop Size**: 48x24" minimum for laptop and mouse, 60x30" standard for dual monitors, 72x30" for spacious multi-monitor setups
- **Memory Presets**: Programmable height settings (2-4 positions) enable quick switching between sitting and standing without manual adjustment
- **Stability**: Steel frames with crossbars minimize wobble at standing height, desktop thickness (0.75-1") affects durability and aesthetics
- **Assembly Requirements**: Full desks need 30-90 minutes assembly, desktop converters ready in minutes, pre-assembled options available at premium

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
