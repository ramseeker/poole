## Key Features to Consider:

- **Sole Type**: Rubber soles handle outdoor trips, EVA foam stays lightweight, memory foam cushions, soft fabric suits indoor-only use.
- **Coverage Style**: Open-toe prevents overheating, closed-toe provides warmth, bootie heights cover ankles, scuff designs slip on easily.
- **Lining Material**: Real sheepskin regulates temperature naturally, faux fur costs less, fleece offers warmth, cotton breathes better.
- **Support Features**: Memory foam molds to feet, arch support benefits plantar fasciitis, orthotic designs accommodate foot conditions.
- **Sizing**: Many run large; size down for snug fit, choose true-to-size for sock wearing, wide widths accommodate bunions and swelling.
- **Maintenance**: Machine-washable simplifies care though may reduce lifespan, spot-cleaning preserves materials, real sheepskin requires special care.
- **Temperature Rating**: Lightweight cotton for warm climates, fleece-lined for moderate cold, shearling and wool for maximum warmth.

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
