## Key Features to Consider:

- **Data Transfer Speed**: USB 3.0 provides 5Gbps, USB 3.2 Gen 2 doubles to 10Gbps, essential for large file transfers and external storage devices.
- **Build Quality**: Aluminum construction offers better durability and heat dissipation than plastic, with premium models rated for 10,000-20,000 plug cycles.
- **Certification**: USB-IF certified adapters guarantee compatibility and safety standards, preventing potential device damage from poor quality alternatives.
- **Charging Support**: Most adapters support data transfer only; check wattage specs if charging capability is required for your use case.
- **Form Factor**: Standard adapters work universally, while right-angle versions excel in tight spaces and reduce cable strain on portable devices.
- **Compatibility**: Verify Thunderbolt 4/3 support for MacBooks, USB-C Power Delivery for fast charging, and OTG functionality for mobile devices.
- **Multi-Pack Value**: Bulk packs of 2-4 adapters provide backups for home, office, and travel at better per-unit pricing.

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
