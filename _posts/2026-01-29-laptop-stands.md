## Key Features to Consider:

- **Height Adjustment Range**: Fixed stands typically raise 4-6 inches, adjustable models offer 2-20 inches for customized ergonomic viewing angles
- **Material Durability**: Aluminum provides best heat dissipation and stability, plastic lighter for portability, wood offers aesthetic warmth
- **Portability vs Stability**: Foldable stands weigh 8oz-2lbs for travel, fixed desk stands 2-5lbs provide rock-solid stability for permanent setups
- **Cooling Ventilation**: Open designs allow passive airflow, active cooling pads with fans reduce laptop temps 10-30Â°F for intensive workloads
- **Weight Capacity**: Most support 2-10 lbs covering 11-17 inch laptops, heavy-duty models handle up to 15 lbs for gaming laptops
- **Cable Management**: Premium stands include routing channels and clips to organize power and peripherals for clean desk aesthetics
- **Angle Flexibility**: Basic stands offer 1-2 preset angles, fully adjustable models provide 0-90 degree tilting for optimal screen positioning

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
