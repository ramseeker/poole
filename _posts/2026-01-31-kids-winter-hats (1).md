## Key Features to Consider:

- **Age Sizing**: Infant (0-12 months), toddler (1-3 years), little kids (4-7 years), big kids (8-14 years) - measure head circumference for best fit
- **Material Warmth**: Fleece-lined provides maximum warmth, acrylic knit standard insulation, wool natural breathable warmth but may itch
- **Ear Coverage**: Beanies cover ears when pulled down, ear flap hats secure with straps, trapper hats offer maximum protection
- **Washability**: Machine washable essential for kids, fleece and acrylic easy care, wool may require hand washing or delicate cycle
- **Safety Features**: Avoid long ties or drawstrings on infant hats, secure chin straps prevent loss, reflective elements improve visibility
- **Character vs Plain**: Licensed characters engage young children, solid colors more versatile and grow with child, pom poms add playful touch
- **Water Resistance**: Water-resistant outer shells protect from snow, fleece lining prevents moisture wicking, quick-dry materials ideal for active kids

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
