## Key Features to Consider:

- **Wi-Fi Standard**: Wi-Fi 7 offers fastest speeds and latest features, Wi-Fi 6E adds 6GHz band, while Wi-Fi 6 provides excellent performance at lower cost for most users
- **Coverage Area**: Match router range to home size - small apartments need 1,500 sq ft coverage, medium homes 2,000-2,500 sq ft, large homes benefit from mesh systems
- **Device Capacity**: Entry routers handle 15-20 devices, mid-range support 50-100 devices, premium models accommodate 200+ simultaneous connections
- **Speed Requirements**: Gigabit plans need routers with 2.5G or higher WAN ports, while 500 Mbps plans work well with standard gigabit Ethernet
- **Mesh Capability**: EasyMesh, AiMesh, or native mesh systems allow seamless expansion for whole-home coverage without dead zones
- **Wired Connectivity**: Consider number and speed of Ethernet ports - gamers and NAS users benefit from multiple 2.5G or 10G ports
- **Advanced Features**: VPN support, parental controls, guest networks, QoS for gaming, and security subscriptions add value based on specific needs

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
