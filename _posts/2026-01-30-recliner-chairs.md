## Key Features to Consider:

- **Recline Mechanism**: Manual pull-tab budget-friendly, power electric smooth operation, infinite-position maximum adjustability
- **Size Specifications**: Standard 35-40" wide fits most, big & tall 42-48" wide for larger users, measure doorways for delivery clearance
- **Upholstery Options**: Leather genuine breathable and ages well, faux leather affordable easy-clean, microfiber soft budget-friendly
- **Wall-Hugger Design**: Standard requires 6-12" clearance, wall-hugger reclines with 4" space, ideal for small rooms
- **Additional Features**: USB charging convenient for devices, massage/heat therapeutic relief, cup holders and storage practical
- **Weight Capacity**: Standard 250-300 lbs adequate, big & tall 350-500 lbs for durability, check specifications for longevity
- **Assembly Requirements**: Most arrive assembled or minimal setup, power recliners require electrical outlet placement

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
