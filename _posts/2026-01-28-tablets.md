## Key Features to Consider:

- **Operating System Choice**: iPadOS offers app optimization and Apple Pencil support, Android provides flexibility and affordability, Fire OS focuses on Amazon content
- **Screen Size Selection**: 7-8" for portability and reading, 10-11" balances mobility and productivity, 12-13" approaches laptop replacement territory
- **Storage Capacity**: 32-64GB sufficient for streaming, 128GB recommended for apps and photos, 256GB+ for professional creative work
- **Performance Tiers**: Budget processors handle streaming and browsing, mid-range chips enable multitasking, premium M-series/flagship chips support desktop tasks
- **Stylus Support**: Apple Pencil (Pro/2) offers best latency, S Pen included free with Samsung, basic capacitive styluses work for notes
- **Connectivity Options**: WiFi-only saves money, cellular adds flexibility for mobile workers, consider 5G future-proofing for cellular models
- **Battery Life Expectations**: Budget tablets deliver 8-10 hours, mid-range 10-13 hours, premium iPads can exceed 10 hours with demanding tasks

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
