## Key Features to Consider:

- **Resolution Standards**: 2560x1080 budget Full HD ultrawide, 3440x1440 UWQHD standard for productivity/gaming, 5120x2160 5K2K for premium professional work
- **Aspect Ratio Options**: 21:9 ultrawide replaces dual monitors, 32:9 super-ultrawide equivalent to two 16:9 displays side-by-side
- **Refresh Rate**: 60-75Hz suits office work, 100-144Hz ideal for gaming, 165-240Hz enables competitive esports advantage
- **Panel Technology**: IPS offers best color accuracy (99% sRGB) and viewing angles, VA provides superior contrast, OLED delivers perfect blacks and response times
- **Curvature Rating**: 1800R moderate curve for 34", 1000R aggressive bend for 49" immersive gaming, flat panels suit productivity workflows
- **Connectivity Needs**: Thunderbolt 3/4 with 90-140W PD charges laptops, DisplayPort 1.4 required for high refresh, USB hub simplifies peripherals
- **HDR Support**: DisplayHDR 400 basic HDR, DisplayHDR 600/1000 noticeable improvement, True Black OLED superior dark scene detail

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
