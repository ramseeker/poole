## Key Features to Consider:

- **Size**: Standard 18x18" for couches; 12x20" lumbar for back support; oversized for floor seating
- **Fill**: Down alternative (hypoallergenic), memory foam (support), polyester (budget-friendly)
- **Cover Material**: Cotton (breathable), velvet (luxury), outdoor fabric (weather-resistant)
- **Insert vs. Cover**: Inserts are complete pillows; covers allow customization and easy washing
- **Outdoor Use**: Look for waterproof, UV-resistant, and mildew-resistant fabrics
- **Care**: Most inserts are machine washable; covers often have zippers for easy removal

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
