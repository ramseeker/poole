## Key Features to Consider:

- **Material Quality**: BPA-free plastic ensures safety for food storage, while heavy-duty construction resists cracking and provides long-term durability
- **Size & Capacity**: Match container size to items - small bins (1-6 qt) for crafts, medium (12-32 qt) for clothes, large (60+ qt) for seasonal storage
- **Lid Security**: Latching lids prevent accidental openings, snap-on lids offer quick access, while airtight seals preserve food freshness
- **Stackability**: Identical-sized containers stack efficiently to maximize vertical space in closets, garages, and pantries
- **Visibility**: Clear plastic allows instant content identification without opening, while solid colors provide privacy for personal items
- **Portability**: Handles and wheels facilitate easy transport, especially important for larger containers and moving applications
- **Versatility**: Multi-purpose containers work across kitchen, closet, garage, and office environments, providing better value than specialized-only options

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
