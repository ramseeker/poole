## Key Features to Consider:

- **Light Color Temperature**: 2700K warm white for relaxation, 4000K neutral for general tasks, 5000-6500K cool white for focus and productivity
- **Brightness Levels**: 300-500 lumens adequate for ambient, 800-1200 lumens task lighting, dimmable offers flexibility for different activities
- **Bulb Type**: LED energy-efficient lasting 25,000+ hours, incandescent warm traditional glow, CFL compact fluorescent budget option
- **Adjustability**: Fixed shades limit direction, swing-arm allows positioning, multi-joint architect lamps offer maximum flexibility
- **Smart Features**: WiFi/Bluetooth app control, voice assistant integration, scheduling automation, color-changing RGB for ambiance
- **Eye Protection**: Flicker-free reduces eye strain, anti-glare diffusers prevent harsh shadows, auto-dimming adjusts to ambient light
- **Style Considerations**: Modern minimalist fits contemporary decor, traditional fabric shades suit classic rooms, industrial metal complements lofts

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
