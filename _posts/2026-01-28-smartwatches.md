## Key Features to Consider:

- **Operating System Compatibility**: Apple Watch requires iPhone, Wear OS works with Android and iPhone, while proprietary systems like Garmin and Fitbit offer cross-platform support
- **Health Tracking Capabilities**: Advanced models include ECG, blood oxygen, sleep analysis, stress monitoring, and even hearing aid features for comprehensive health management
- **Battery Life Expectations**: Budget models last 1-3 days, mid-range 5-7 days, while Garmin solar models can achieve weeks or infinite battery with proper sun exposure
- **Display Technology**: AMOLED provides vibrant colors and deep blacks, always-on displays add convenience, while high brightness (2,000-3,000 nits) ensures outdoor visibility
- **Fitness and Sports Features**: GPS accuracy, heart rate zones, workout detection, running coaching, and sport-specific modes vary widely between basic and premium models
- **Smart Features Integration**: Call answering, voice assistants (Alexa, Siri, Google Assistant), music storage, contactless payments, and app ecosystems enhance daily utility
- **Durability and Water Resistance**: 5 ATM suitable for swimming, 10 ATM for water sports, while military-grade and titanium construction ensure longevity in harsh conditions

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
