## Key Features to Consider:

- **Portability Design**: Tri-fold keyboards compact to pocket size, bi-fold balance portability and typing, full-size sacrifice size for comfort
- **Battery Life**: Rechargeable batteries convenient but require charging every few weeks/months, replaceable AAA last 1-2 years but add weight
- **Multi-Device Switching**: Easy-Switch buttons enable instant device changes, single-device keyboards require re-pairing to switch connections
- **Key Travel**: Full-size keys with 1.5-2mm travel feel like laptop keyboards, low-profile scissor switches quieter and faster response
- **Compatibility**: Universal keyboards work across iOS, Android, Windows, device-specific optimize features for particular ecosystems
- **Size vs Typing**: Full-size keyboards (11-12" wide) offer best typing, compact 9-10" balance portability and usability, folding sacrifice comfort
- **Connectivity**: Bluetooth 5.0+ offers reliable 30ft range and lower power consumption, 2.4GHz dongles more stable but occupy USB port

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
