## Key Features to Consider:

- **Cable Length Selection**: 3ft ideal for power banks and car charging, 6ft standard for home use, 10ft+ perfect for bedside charging and larger rooms
- **Charging Speed Ratings**: Look for 20W+ for fast phone charging, 60-100W for laptops, with USB-C PD (Power Delivery) enabling fastest speeds
- **Durability Construction**: Nylon braided cables resist tangling and last 10x longer than standard, with reinforced strain relief preventing connector breakage
- **Connector Compatibility**: USB-C to USB-C for modern devices, Lightning for iPhones, Micro USB for older Android, 3-in-1 cables offer universal convenience
- **MFi Certification**: Apple MFi (Made for iPhone/iPad) certification ensures compatibility, safety, and reliability with iOS devices without error messages
- **Data Transfer Speeds**: USB 2.0 handles 480 Mbps sufficient for charging, USB 3.0/3.1 delivers 5-10 Gbps for fast file transfers between devices
- **Cable Thickness**: Thicker gauge wires (20AWG vs 28AWG) deliver more power efficiently with less voltage drop, crucial for fast charging and long cables
