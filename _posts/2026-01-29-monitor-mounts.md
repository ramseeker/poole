## Key Features to Consider:

- **Weight Capacity**: Budget mounts handle 17-22 lbs (27" monitors), mid-range 25-30 lbs (32" displays), premium mounts support 33-50 lbs (ultrawide/large screens)
- **VESA Compatibility**: Most monitors use 75x75mm or 100x100mm patterns, larger displays require 200x200mm or 400x400mm mounting standards
- **Articulation Range**: Basic mounts offer tilt only, mid-range add swivel/rotate, premium gas-spring arms provide effortless repositioning in all directions
- **Desk Mounting**: C-clamps fit 0.4-3.5" thick desks quickly, grommet mounts require drilling but offer cleaner aesthetic and stability
- **Cable Management**: Integrated channels route cables from monitor to desk, keeping workspace organized and preventing tangling behind displays
- **Height Adjustment**: Post-based stands offer 6-12" adjustment, gas springs provide smooth infinite positioning within 17" vertical range
- **Monitor Configuration**: Single arms suit solo displays, dual side-by-side for productivity, vertical stacking saves desk width, 3-4 mounts for trading/content creation

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
