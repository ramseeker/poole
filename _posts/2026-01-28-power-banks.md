## Key Features to Consider:

- **Capacity Requirements**: 5,000-10,000mAh charges phones 1-2 times, 20,000mAh handles 4-5 charges, while 50,000+ mAh powers multiple devices for days
- **Charging Speed Technology**: Look for PD (Power Delivery) 45W+ for laptops, 22.5W for fast phone charging, while 15W wireless MagSafe offers convenience
- **Port Configuration**: Multiple USB-C ports enable simultaneous charging, built-in cables eliminate forgetting cords, while wireless charging adds convenience
- **Portability vs Power**: Slim 5,000-10,000mAh models fit pockets, mid-size 20,000mAh balance capacity and weight, while 50,000+ mAh suits camping and extended trips
- **MagSafe Compatibility**: Magnetic attachment for iPhone 12-17 series offers hands-free charging, with Qi2 certification ensuring 15W fast wireless charging speeds
- **Travel-Friendly Features**: Flight-approved ratings (typically under 100Wh), built-in AC plugs for wall charging, and LED displays showing remaining battery percentage
- **Durability and Safety**: Quality brands include overcharge protection, temperature control, and short-circuit prevention with 500+ charge cycle lifespans

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
