## Key Features to Consider:

- **Point Size**: Fine (0.5-0.7mm) for detailed work, Medium (1.0mm) for everyday use, Bold (1.4-1.6mm) for emphasis
- **Ink Type**: Hybrid ballpoint (Jetstream, Acroball) combines smoothness of gel with reliability of oil-based
- **Grip**: Rubber grips reduce hand fatigue during extended writing
- **Retractable vs. Cap**: Retractable more convenient; cap protects tip better
- **Refillable**: Eco-friendly and cost-effective for premium pens
- **Quick-Dry**: Important for left-handed writers to prevent smudging

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
