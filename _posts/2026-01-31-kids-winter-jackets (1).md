## Key Features to Consider:

- **Insulation Type**: Down provides best warmth-to-weight ratio, synthetic maintains warmth when wet, fleece breathable for active kids
- **Fill Power**: 550-fill adequate for moderate cold, 650-700 fill for very cold, 800+ fill premium lightweight warmth
- **Waterproof Rating**: 5K adequate for light snow, 10K standard winter protection, 15K+ for heavy snow and skiing
- **Growth Features**: Adjustable cuffs extend sleeves, room-to-grow systems add 1-2 inches, buy one size up for longevity
- **Safety Elements**: Reflective strips improve visibility, bright colors easier to spot, ID labels inside jacket helpful
- **Layering System**: 3-in-1 jackets offer maximum versatility, shell + fleece combinations adapt to temperatures, base layers underneath
- **Activity Level**: Heavy insulation for sedentary play, lighter fill for active kids who generate heat, vents prevent overheating

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
