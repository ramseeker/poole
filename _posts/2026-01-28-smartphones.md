## Key Features to Consider:

- **Operating System Choice**: iOS offers seamless Apple ecosystem integration and long software support, while Android provides customization flexibility and diverse price points
- **Camera System Quality**: Flagship phones feature 48-200MP sensors with computational photography, mid-range offers 50MP capable systems, budget phones provide basic 13-50MP cameras
- **5G Connectivity**: Essential for future-proofing, with mmWave for fastest speeds in cities and sub-6GHz for broader coverage and better battery life
- **Battery Life Expectations**: Flagships typically last full day with heavy use, mid-range phones often exceed 24 hours, while budget models with larger batteries can reach 2-3 days
- **Storage Capacity**: 128GB minimum recommended, 256GB ideal for photo/video users, 512GB+ for content creators, with cloud storage reducing local needs
- **Display Technology**: AMOLED/OLED provide vibrant colors and deep blacks, LCD remains budget standard, with 90-120Hz refresh rates improving scrolling smoothness
- **Processor Performance**: Apple A-series and Snapdragon 8 Gen chips lead performance, mid-range Tensor and Snapdragon 7 series handle daily tasks, budget chips suit basic use

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
