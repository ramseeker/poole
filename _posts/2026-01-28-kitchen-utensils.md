## Key Features to Consider:

- **Heat Resistance**: Look for 446°F-600°F rating for silicone utensils
- **Material**: Silicone (non-stick safe), wood (natural, gentle), stainless steel (durable)
- **BPA-Free**: Essential for food-grade silicone products
- **Dishwasher Safe**: Most silicone and stainless steel options are dishwasher safe
- **Set vs. Individual**: Sets offer better value; buy individual pieces for specific needs
- **Handle Design**: Wooden handles stay cool; stainless can get hot

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
