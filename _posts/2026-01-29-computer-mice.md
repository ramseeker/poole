## Key Features to Consider:

- **Sensor Technology**: Optical sensors work on most surfaces with 800-12,000 DPI for everyday use, laser sensors offer 16,000-30,000+ DPI for gaming precision
- **Wireless vs Wired**: Wireless (Bluetooth/2.4GHz) offers desk freedom with 1-24 month battery life, wired eliminates latency for competitive gaming
- **Ergonomic Design**: Vertical mice reduce wrist strain by 10-50%, contoured shapes support natural hand positions, trackballs eliminate arm movement
- **DPI Adjustability**: 800-1200 DPI suits general use, 1600-3200 for graphics work, 8,000-30,000 for gaming with switchable sensitivity levels
- **Programmable Buttons**: 2-button mice for basic use, 5-8 buttons for productivity shortcuts, 12-20 buttons for MMO gaming and complex macros
- **Battery Life Considerations**: Budget mice use AA/AAA batteries (3-12 months), premium rechargeable USB-C last 1-3 months, gaming mice 40-280 hours
- **Connectivity Options**: Bluetooth 5.0+ for multi-device switching, 2.4GHz dongles for reliable connection, wired USB for zero-latency gaming

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
