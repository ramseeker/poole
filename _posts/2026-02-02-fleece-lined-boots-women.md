## Key Features to Consider:

- **Insulation Amount**: 200g suits moderate cold (20-40F), 400g handles severe cold (0-20F), 600g+ for extreme conditions (-20F and below).
- **Waterproofing**: Full waterproof construction essential for snow and slush, water-resistant coatings sufficient for dry cold and light precipitation.
- **Shaft Height**: Short booties offer versatility, mid-calf prevents snow entry, tall shafts provide maximum coverage and warmth.
- **Entry Style**: Lace-up provides customizable fit, slip-on offers convenience, side zippers combine secure fit with easy entry.
- **Sole Construction**: Rubber outsoles grip ice and snow, vibram treads offer maximum traction, lightweight EVA reduces fatigue.
- **Temperature Rating**: Check manufacturer ratings; most boots comfortable 10-15 degrees above listed minimum for stationary activities.
- **Fleece Type**: Full fleece lining provides maximum warmth, partial lining reduces bulk, removable liners allow drying and washing.

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
