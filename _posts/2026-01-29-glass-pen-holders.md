## Key Features to Consider:

- **Material Quality**: Acrylic offers clarity and durability at lower cost, genuine glass provides premium weight and scratch resistance
- **Compartment Configuration**: Single cups suit minimalist setups, 3-5 compartments organize pens by type, drawers store small supplies
- **Size Dimensions**: 3-4" diameter adequate for basic use, larger 5-6" holds scissors and rulers, height 4-5" prevents tipping
- **Base Stability**: Weighted bottoms prevent tipping from tall items, wider bases increase stability, rubber feet protect desk surfaces
- **Aesthetic Match**: Clear suits any decor, metallic mesh adds industrial style, decorative patterns complement themed office spaces
- **Capacity Planning**: Standard holds 20-30 pens, larger compartments accommodate 50+ writing instruments and office tools
- **Cleaning Ease**: Smooth glass/acrylic wipes clean easily, mesh requires more effort, removable compartments simplify maintenance

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
