## Key Features to Consider:

- **Product Line Positioning**: Spectre ultra-premium innovation, Envy premium consumer, Pavilion mainstream value, EliteBook business durability
- **Audio Quality**: Bang & Olufsen speakers on Spectre/Envy models provide superior sound, dual speakers standard on Pavilion
- **Security Features**: EliteBook Wolf Pro Security, HP Sure View privacy screens, TPM 2.0 encryption, fingerprint/IR camera authentication
- **Build Materials**: Spectre gem-cut aluminum, Envy metal/plastic hybrid, Pavilion plastic construction, EliteBook magnesium alloy
- **Display Options**: OLED on premium models, IPS standard, privacy screens on business, touch/stylus on convertibles
- **Battery Life**: Spectre/Envy 10-14 hours, Pavilion 7-9 hours, gaming 4-6 hours, long-life options on business models
- **Warranty & Support**: 1-year standard, Care Packs extend to 3-5 years, next-business-day available for business laptops

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
