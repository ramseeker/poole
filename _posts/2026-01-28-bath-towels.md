## Key Features to Consider:

- **GSM (Grams per Square Meter)**: Higher GSM (600-700+) = thicker, more absorbent, and plushier towels
- **Material**: Turkish and Egyptian cotton are premium choices for absorbency and softness
- **Size**: Standard bath towels are 27"x54", bath sheets are 35"x70" or larger
- **Quick-Dry**: Waffle weave and microfiber options dry faster than traditional cotton
- **Care**: Most cotton towels get softer with washing; avoid fabric softener for maximum absorbency

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
