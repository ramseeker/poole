## Key Features to Consider:

- **Material Options**: Acrylic affordable and easy care, wool naturally warm and breathable, fleece soft and moisture-wicking, merino premium temperature regulation
- **Fit Styles**: Fitted beanies stay in place during activity, slouchy relaxed casual look, cuffed adds extra ear coverage
- **Warmth Level**: Single-layer adequate for mild cold, fleece-lined for moderate cold, insulated/down for extreme conditions
- **Ear Coverage**: Standard beanies cover ears when pulled down, ear flap hats offer maximum protection, trapper styles secure with straps
- **Activity Purpose**: Running hats lightweight and moisture-wicking, work hats durable with hi-vis options, casual fashion-focused designs
- **Care Instructions**: Machine washable convenient for regular use, hand wash preserves wool and delicate fabrics, air dry prevents shrinking
- **Head Size**: Most beanies stretch to fit, measure circumference (21-24" adult average) for precise fit, adjustable trappers accommodate various sizes

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
