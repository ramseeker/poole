## Key Features to Consider:

- **Tip Styles**: Chisel tips create broad 3-5mm or narrow 1-2mm lines, fine tips offer precision underlining, blade tips allow seeing text
- **Ink Formulation**: Fluorescent inks highly visible on white paper, pastel mildliners reduce eye strain, gel formula prevents bleed-through on thin pages
- **Color Selection**: Yellow standard and most visible, multi-color sets enable color-coding systems, pastel palettes aesthetic for journaling
- **Bleed-Through Prevention**: Standard highlighters suitable for regular paper, gel or Bible highlighters required for thin or bible pages
- **Portability**: Retractable designs pocket-safe without caps, click mechanisms convenient, cap highlighters need organization
- **Durability**: Quality brands last 400-800 meters of highlighting, budget options 200-400 meters, anti-dry technology extends uncapped life
- **Special Features**: Erasable highlighters remove mistakes, double-ended provide two tips, window tips show text while highlighting

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
