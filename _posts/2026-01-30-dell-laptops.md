## Key Features to Consider:

- **Product Line Differences**: XPS premium consumer, Inspiron budget-friendly home, Latitude business durability, Precision professional workstations
- **Build Quality**: XPS aluminum/carbon fiber premium, Inspiron plastic affordable, Latitude magnesium alloy military-grade, Precision workstation certified
- **Display Options**: FHD (1920x1080) standard, FHD+ (1920x1200) taller aspect, QHD/UHD for creative work, OLED vibrant colors
- **Performance Tiers**: Core i3/Celeron basic tasks, Core i5/i7 mainstream productivity, Core i9/Ultra 7 content creation and gaming
- **Business Features**: Latitude includes TPM 2.0 security, fingerprint readers, MIL-STD durability, vPro management for IT departments
- **Warranty & Support**: Consumer 1-year standard, business 3-year available, ProSupport for next-day on-site service upgrades
- **Upgradeability**: Inspiron often soldered RAM, XPS limited upgrades, Latitude/Precision offer RAM/storage expansion options

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
