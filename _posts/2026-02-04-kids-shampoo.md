## Key Features to Consider:

- **Tear-Free Formula**: Essential for stress-free bath time. Look for "no more tears" or "tear-free" labeling to ensure the formula won't irritate eyes if it accidentally gets in during washing.

- **Hypoallergenic & Dermatologist-Tested**: Choose products tested on sensitive skin and free from common irritants. Hypoallergenic formulas minimize the risk of allergic reactions and skin sensitivity.

- **Free From Harsh Chemicals**: Avoid parabens, sulfates, phthalates, synthetic fragrances, and dyes. These additives can be harsh on delicate skin and hair. Natural and plant-based ingredients are gentler alternatives.

- **2-in-1 vs Single-Purpose**: 2-in-1 shampoo and body washes offer convenience for busy parents and younger children. Older kids with longer or textured hair may benefit from separate shampoo and conditioner for better manageability.

- **Hair Type Specific**: Consider your child's hair texture. Curly or coily hair benefits from moisturizing formulas with shea butter or natural oils. Fine, straight hair needs lighter formulas that won't weigh it down.

- **Specialty Needs**: If your child has cradle cap, eczema, dandruff, or other scalp conditions, look for medicated or therapeutic formulas specifically designed to address these issues while remaining gentle.
