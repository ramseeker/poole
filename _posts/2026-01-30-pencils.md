## Key Features to Consider:

- **Lead Hardness**: #2 HB standard for tests and writing, 2B-4B softer for sketching, H-2H harder for technical drawing
- **Wood Quality**: Cedar wood sharpens smoothly and resists splintering, basswood budget alternative, incense cedar premium option
- **Eraser Quality**: Latex-free prevents allergies, PVC-free environmentally friendly, replaceable on mechanical pencils extends life
- **Mechanical Lead Size**: 0.5mm fine for detailed work, 0.7mm standard versatility, 0.9mm durability for heavy writing
- **Break Resistance**: Bonded lead cores resist breakage 3x better, thicker mechanical leads less fragile, quality wood prevents splitting
- **Grip Comfort**: Soft rubber grips reduce hand fatigue, hexagonal barrels prevent rolling, cushioned grips for extended writing
- **Value Considerations**: Wood pencils cost $0.05-0.30 each in bulk, mechanical pencils $0.50-3.00 reusable with refill lead

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
