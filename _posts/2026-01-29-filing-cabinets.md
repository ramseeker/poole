## Key Features to Consider:

- **Drawer Configuration**: 2-drawer suits compact spaces with basic needs, 3-4 drawer vertical for extensive files, lateral cabinets maximize width efficiency
- **File Size Compatibility**: Letter size (8.5x11) standard for most offices, legal (8.5x14) requires deeper drawers, some models accommodate both formats
- **Locking Mechanisms**: Core removable locks secure all drawers simultaneously, individual locks offer selective access, anti-tilt prevents drawer accidents
- **Slide Quality**: Ball-bearing full-extension slides allow 100% drawer access and smooth operation, cheaper models use basic runners limiting accessibility
- **Mobility Options**: Stationary cabinets provide stable storage, caster wheels enable flexible office layouts, locking wheels prevent unwanted movement
- **Material Construction**: Steel offers fire-resistance and durability (300-500 lbs capacity), wood provides aesthetic warmth but lower weight capacity
- **Dimensions Planning**: Vertical cabinets save floor space (15-18W, 52H), lateral units maximize drawer space (30-42W, 28-30H) for large document volumes

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
