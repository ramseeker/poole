## Key Features to Consider:

- **Accuracy Range**: Budget sensors ±3-5% humidity accuracy adequate for home comfort, ±2% suits plant care, ±1% required for cigars and instruments
- **Response Time**: Fast 10-second updates for real-time monitoring, slower 30-60 second intervals conserve battery in always-on displays
- **Connectivity Options**: Standalone displays simple and affordable, Bluetooth enables smartphone tracking, WiFi allows remote monitoring and alerts
- **Display Features**: Large LCD screens easy to read from distance, backlit displays visible in darkness, E-ink consume minimal power
- **Data Logging**: Memory stores min/max readings for trends, app-connected sensors offer unlimited cloud storage with export capabilities
- **Placement Flexibility**: Magnetic backs attach to metal surfaces, kickstands for tabletop, wall mounts for permanent installation
- **Battery Life**: Basic displays last 6-12 months on coin cells, smart sensors 3-6 months, E-ink models achieve 1-2 years per charge

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
