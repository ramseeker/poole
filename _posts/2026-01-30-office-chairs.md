## Key Features to Consider:

- **Lumbar Support**: Fixed lumbar adequate for most, adjustable height/depth customizes fit, dynamic support adapts to movement throughout day
- **Seat Cushion**: Memory foam conforms to body, high-density foam maintains shape longer, mesh breathable for hot climates
- **Armrest Adjustment**: Fixed arms save money, height-adjustable basic ergonomics, 4D arms (height/width/depth/angle) maximize customization
- **Recline Mechanism**: Tilt lock fixes position, synchro-tilt coordinates seat and back, multi-position allows various recline angles
- **Weight Capacity**: Standard 250 lbs adequate for most, big & tall 350-400 lbs for larger users, check specifications for durability
- **Warranty Coverage**: Budget chairs 1-2 years, mid-range 5-7 years, premium Herman Miller/Steelcase 10-12 years with comprehensive coverage
- **Height Range**: Standard 16-20" seat height suits 5'4"-6'2", shorter/taller users need specific adjustments, check min/max specifications

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
