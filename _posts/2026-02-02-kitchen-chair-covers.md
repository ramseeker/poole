## Key Features to Consider:

- **Coverage Type**: Full covers protect entire chair including back, while seat-only versions offer easier installation and lower cost.
- **Waterproofing**: Essential for families with children or frequent entertaining, though breathability may be compromised compared to fabric-only options.
- **Fabric Material**: Spandex/polyester blends offer maximum stretch, velvet adds luxury, while jacquard patterns provide visual texture and elegance.
- **Fit Range**: Check measurements carefully - most fit seats 16-20 inches wide with back heights 19-24 inches for parsons-style chairs.
- **Installation Method**: Elastic bands and sewn-in elastics secure better than loose-fitting covers, preventing constant readjustment during meals.
- **Washability**: Machine-washable covers simplify maintenance, though some materials like velvet may require gentle cycles or hand washing.
- **Color Selection**: Neutral tones (beige, gray, khaki) hide stains better than white while coordinating with varied decor schemes.

All prices are approximate and may vary based on sales and availability.
