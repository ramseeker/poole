## Key Features to Consider:

- **Washability**: Machine-washable rugs simplify cleaning for homes with pets, children, or high traffic, though large sizes (8x10+) require commercial washers.
- **Pile Height**: Low-pile (0.25 inch) rugs fit under doors and vacuum easily, while high-pile shag rugs (1+ inch) offer luxury softness.
- **Material**: Synthetic fibers resist stains and cost less, while natural fibers like wool and jute provide premium texture and durability.
- **Backing Type**: Non-slip rubber backing prevents sliding on hard floors, though rug pads offer superior grip and floor protection.
- **Pattern & Color**: Light colors brighten spaces but show dirt, dark colors hide stains but may make rooms feel smaller.
- **Size Selection**: Measure your space allowing 18-24 inches between rug edges and walls for proper proportions in living areas.
- **Indoor/Outdoor Use**: Polypropylene and recycled plastic rugs withstand moisture and UV exposure for patios and covered porches.
